Rolex

Sea Dweller Deepsea

Movement: Rolex Automatic

Case: 43mm

Retail: $12,050.00

Artisan
Newly
Added

The Rolex Deepsea was designed for extreme underwater exploration. An intricate three-piece case architecture, the Ringlock System, enables it to withstand the colossal pressure at depths of up to 3,900 metres (12,000 feet). The Rolex Deepsea has a bezel inlaid with a virtually scratch resistant black Cerachrom disc and a titanium caseback. The Rolex Deepsea is also equipped with a unique bracelet, featuring a double extension system for a perfect fit over any diving suit.
